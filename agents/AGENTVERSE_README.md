# SleepSense AI

SleepSense AI analyses sleep data from EEG recordings and wearable devices (Apple Health, Fitbit, Garmin) and answers natural-language questions about your sleep health.

## What I can do

- Tell you how you slept last night — quality score, efficiency, REM, deep sleep, awakenings
- Identify sleep trends and sleep debt over the past week
- Cross-reference your morning voice health check (fatigue, stress, cognitive load) with last night's sleep
- Suggest concrete improvements based on AASM sleep medicine guidelines

## Example prompts

- "How did I sleep last night?"
- "What was my sleep efficiency this week?"
- "Am I getting enough deep sleep?"
- "How much sleep debt do I have?"
- "Is my sleep schedule consistent?"
- "Why do I feel tired today?"
- "What time should I go to bed tonight?"
- "How do my fatigue scores relate to my sleep?"
